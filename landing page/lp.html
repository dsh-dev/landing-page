🎨 About Me

👋 Hello! I'm Dimple, a passionate self-taught artist who loves to bring imagination to life through ✏️ pencil, 🖋️ ink, and 💻 digital brushes. On my YouTube channel, I share ⏱️ time-lapse drawings, 🖼️ portrait sketches, and 🎨 creative art experiments to inspire others and express my artistic journey.

🧠 I believe that art is a powerful tool for ✨ self-expression and 🌿 self-reflection. Whether it's a simple doodle or an intricate masterpiece, every line tells a story 📖. Join me as I explore different styles 🖌️, techniques 🧵, and challenges 🎯—all while staying true to the joy of creating 💖.
